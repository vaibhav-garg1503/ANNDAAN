How It Works

Donate Food

Submit extra food from your hostel or home.

We Collect

Our volunteers come and collect the food.

We Distribute

We deliver it to orphanages, old age homes, etc.

Our Vision

To create a community where no food goes to waste and every individual has access to nutritious meals,
fostering a culture of sustainability and compassion in India.

Our Mission

To efficiently collect surplus food from hostels, Hotels, Restaurant and redistribute it to orphanages, food banks, and old age homes,
ensuring that excess food serves those in need while reducing food wastage

How We Operate

We work with food donors and recipients to ensure that surplus food reaches those who need it most.

The Need

Millions of people go hungry every day while perfectly good food goes to waste. Our service bridges this gap.

The Impact of Food Waste

Food waste contributes to environmental degradation. By redirecting surplus food, we help mitigate this impact.
