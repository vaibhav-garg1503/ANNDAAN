Who Are We

Since 2025, ANNDAAN Food Donation System has been working to stop food waste by partnering with hostels and other food providers. We collect extra food that would otherwise go to waste and give it to orphanages, food banks, and old age homes, making sure people in need get healthy meals. Till now, we’ve saved over 10,000 pounds of good food, supporting both the environment and the community. Our team is trained in food safety and management, and we are dedicated to turning extra food into hope and help for others.

What We Do

At ANNDAAN Food Donation System, we run a powerful food donation program focused on reducing food waste and feeding those in need. We partner with hotels, restaurants, and marriage venues, where large amounts of surplus food are often wasted. Instead of letting it go to waste, we collect this food and distribute it to orphanages, food banks, and old age homes.
We work closely with trusted NGOs to make sure the food reaches the right people quickly and safely. Our system includes proper food handling, easy communication, and transparent reporting to maintain high standards.
Our funding is sustainable — we use a portion of our donor partners' tax benefits to run and grow our mission, without needing extra donations. We are now working to expand our network and build strong Harvest Programs with more hotels and event organizers.
